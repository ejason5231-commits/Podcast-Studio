Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 2025

Daily Vocab uses Firebase Authentication to store user accounts. You can request your account and all related data to be deleted at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

1. Inside the App (if available)

If the app includes a "Delete Account" button, you can delete your data directly.

2. Request Deletion by Email

You may send an email to request deletion:

Email: your-email@example.com

Please include:

  • Your account email
  • Your user ID (optional)

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • Your profile (email, name, photo)
  • Any saved learning progress (if stored)

What Will NOT Be Deleted Automatically

  • Aggregated anonymous analytics data (not linked to your identity)
  • Advertising identifiers managed by Google Play Services
